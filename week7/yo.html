        • Desolation Wilderness
          Hiking
          South Lake Tahoe, CA 96158
          7/25/2015

          Loved this place and would definitely come back here next year. We went for two days backpacking trip to lake Lois. The trail was very well maintained and had decent amount of signs along the way. Came there on Saturday morning and found no parking at the entire parking lot. We should have checked that there is a wildflower guided walk every Saturday at 10am during summer time. We parked a bit down the road which added another mile to our trip but wasn't too much of a problem. One thing to note: they don't have bear boxes at the parking lot so plan not to leave anything scented in the car.
